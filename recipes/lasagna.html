World's Best Lasagna

Better than your Meemaw's dried-out abomination that you ate for your parent's nostalgia. Worse than the restaurants named after real Italians.

Ingredients

  • Meat: This super meaty lasagna has sweet Italian sausage and lean ground beef.
  • Onion and garlic: An onion and two gloves of garlic are cooked with the meat to add flavor.
  • Tomato products: You'll need a can of crushed tomatoes, two cans of tomato sauce, and two cans of tomato paste.
  • Sugar: Two tablesppons of white sugar add subtle sweetness and enhance the flavor of the sauce.
  • Spices and seasonings: Fresh parsley, dried basil leaves, saly, Italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and black pepper.
  • Lasagna noodles: Store-bought or homemade
  • Cheeses: Parmesan, mozzarella, and ricotta cheese make this lasagna extra decadent.
  • Egg: An egg helps bind the rocotta so it doesn't ooze out of the lasagna when you cut into it.

Step-By-Step Instructions

Here's a very brief overview of what you can expect when you make homemade lasagna

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
